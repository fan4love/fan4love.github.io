What to read when you are not working

—不工作时读什么

Summer is in the air. People in the northern hemisphere are starting to discuss holiday plans and making some bold wardrobe choices. Recommendations for beach reads are coming out left, right and centre. The oddest of such lists are those aimed at the relaxed executive.

空气中弥漫着夏天的气息。北半球里的人们开始讨论度假计划，并做出一些大胆的衣着选择。海滩阅读的建议从左边，右边和中间出来。这类名单中最奇怪的是那些针对放松管理者的名单。

Each summer JPMorgan Chase’s wealth managers release a reading list. Their recommendations for 2022 include a book by a bunch of McKinsey consultants on ceo excellence and a comprehensive guide to non-fungible tokens. You can almost smell the sun-tan lotion. This year’s reading list is also available to explore in the metaverse, because nothing says the azure waters of the Mediterranean like choosing an avatar.

每年夏天，摩根大通(JPMorgan Chase)的理财经理都会发布一份阅读清单。他们对2022年的建议包括一本由麦肯锡(McKinsey)一群顾问撰写的关于 CEO 卓越的书，以及一本关于不可替代代币的全面指南。你几乎可以闻到防晒油的味道。今年的阅读清单也可以在元宇宙中探索，因为没有什么比选择一个阿凡达更能体现地中海蔚蓝的海水了。

In its pick of summer business books, the Financial Times has chosen titles that range from hybrid work to the pitfalls of strategy. hr Exchange Network, a news site, encourages its readers to lounge on the beach with a copy of the “Essential hr Handbook”—and appears not to be joking. It is only a matter of time before The Economist does something similar.

英国《金融时报》在其精选的暑期商业图书中，选择了从混合工作到战略陷阱的各种书籍。新闻网站 hr Exchange Network 鼓励读者拿着一本《人力资源基本手册》在海滩上休息ーー这似乎不是在开玩笑。《经济学人》采取类似措施只是时间问题。

People should read whatever they want. The books on the list may well be useful: no mosquito would survive contact with the “Essential hr Handbook”. But anything that contains the words “blockchain” or “McKinsey” is missing the point. Plenty of people spend the majority of their waking hours either working or thinking about work. The idea of a summer read is that it should provide an escape from the office, not yet another way to think about it.

人们想读什么就读什么。清单上的书籍可能很有用: 没有蚊子会生存接触“基本人力资源手册”。但是，任何包含“区块链”或“麦肯锡”这些词的东西都没有抓住重点。很多人醒着的大部分时间要么在工作，要么在思考工作。暑期阅读的理念是，它应该提供一种逃离办公室的方式，而不是另一种思考方式。

In an ideal world people would pack several P.G. Wodehouses and switch off entirely. But publishers could also do their bit and release titles that really are meant to be beach reads on business. These books would be aimed at the off-duty person behind the Zoom screen. They would contain precisely no tips on productivity gains and extol inactivity over frenzy. Instead of showing you “how you too can model yourself on the very best”, as the book on successful chief executives allegedly will, summer titles should give you permission to fall asleep in a pool of your own dribble. Here, then, are a few suggestions to get the industry thinking.

在一个理想的世界里，人们会带上几个 P.G.Wodehouse，然后完全关掉。但是出版商也可以尽自己的一份力量，发布那些真正意义上的商业海滩读物。这些书的目标读者是放大屏幕后的下班人员。它们不会包含任何提高生产率的建议，也不会赞美不作为的狂热。与其向你展示“你如何也能以最优秀的人为榜样”(这本关于成功首席执行官的书据称就是这么做的) ，暑期读物应该允许你在自己的口水池里睡着。这里有一些建议，可以让业界思考一下。

In 2005 two insead professors, W. Chan Kim and Renée Mauborgne, wrote a book called “Blue Ocean Strategy”, which divided marketplaces into uncontested areas (the “blue ocean”) and those infested by predatory competitors (the “red ocean”). But what if you don’t really fancy getting in the water at all? “Yellow Sand Strategy” makes the case that sometimes the best thing to do is remain entirely inactive and hope that nothing bad happens. (“Yellow Ocean Strategy” is a different book entirely, for executives who do things so incompetently that no one gives them any extra work.)

2005年，欧洲工商管理学院的两位教授，W.Chan Kim 和 Renée Mauborgne，写了一本名为《蓝海战略》的书，书中将市场划分为无争议的区域(“蓝海”)和被掠夺性竞争者出没的区域(“红海”)。但如果你根本不想下水呢？“黄沙战略”认为，有时候最好的办法就是完全不采取行动，希望不会发生什么不好的事情。(《黄海战略》(Yellow Ocean Strategy)是一本完全不同的书，针对的是那些做事不称职、以至于没人给他们增加任何额外工作的高管。)

The United States Marine Corps has a practice of having senior officers serve up meals to junior members of the unit as a way of cementing bonds. That habit lay behind the title of a management bestseller published by Simon Sinek called “Leaders Eat Last”. On holiday, though, you don’t have to build morale or worry about your team. Read “Leaders Eat Three Club Sandwiches In a Row and Need to Have a Short Lie-Down”, and feel better about yourself.

美国海军陆战队有一种做法，即让高级军官为部队初级成员提供膳食，以此巩固关系。西蒙•西尼克(Simon Sinek)出版的一本管理类畅销书《领导者最后吃饭》(Leadership Eat Last)就是基于这种习惯。不过，在假期里，你不必鼓舞士气，也不必担心你的团队。阅读“领导者连续吃三个俱乐部三明治，需要短暂的躺下休息”，并且对自己感觉更好。

In “The Innovator’s Dilemma” Clayton Christensen describes how leaders of established firms often fail to take advantage of new technologies and risk letting scrappy startups turn into formidable rivals as a result. But the summer break is no time to be thinking about disruption of any kind. Instead, turn your mind to more prosaic problems. “The Procrastinator’s Dilemma” looks at the difficult choice people face between letting work pile up until it really has to be done or letting work pile up until it really, really has to be done.

在《创新者的困境》一书中，克莱顿 · 克里斯滕森描述了老牌公司的领导者们如何经常无法利用新技术，从而冒险让好斗的初创公司变成可怕的竞争对手。但是暑假不是考虑任何破坏的时候。相反，把你的思想转向更平凡的问题。“拖延者的困境”考察了人们面临的困难选择: 是让工作堆积起来直到它真的必须完成，还是让工作堆积起来直到它真的，真的必须完成。

The closer you look, the more you realise that underachievers and rank amateurs are badly served by business publishers. There is a market for laziness: the success of “The 4-Hour Work Week”, by Tim Ferriss, was no accident. With just a few tweaks here and there, many entries in the back catalogue of business bestsellers become ripe for the beach. From “Seven Habits of Highly Ineffective People” to “Start with Why Should I” and “What Colour is Your Sun Lounger?”, the possibilities are endless.

你看得越仔细，就越会意识到，商业出版商对那些表现不佳的业余人士和排名靠前的业余人士的服务很差。懒惰是有市场的: 蒂姆•费里斯(Tim Ferriss)的《每周工作4小时》(The 4-Hour Work Week)的成功绝非偶然。只要稍作调整，许多商业畅销书目录中的许多作品就可以在海滩上畅销了。从“高效率人士的七个习惯”到“我为什么应该开始”和“你的太阳躺椅是什么颜色?”可能性是无限的。

These are not the sort of titles anyone wants to be seen reading at work or posting about on LinkedIn. There are no bragging rights associated with them. But the beach is a place to unwind. If ever there is a time for reading lists to indulge the unmotivated and celebrate indolence, the summer is it.

这些不是那种任何人都希望在工作中看到或在 LinkedIn 上发布的标题。他们没有任何吹嘘的资本。但是海滩是一个放松的地方。如果说有一个时间可以让阅读清单放纵自己，庆祝自己的懒惰，那就是夏天。

Amazon, Apple, Meta, JPMorgan Chase, Microsoft and Nike were among those that pledged to cover travel costs for employees seeking abortions and other medical care not available in their state.

几家美国公司对最高法院推翻罗伊诉韦德案(Roe v Wade)的裁决做出了回应。罗伊诉韦德案于1973年宣布堕胎是一项宪法权利。亚马逊(Amazon)、苹果(Apple)、梅塔(Meta)、摩根大通(JPMorgan Chase)、微软(Microsoft)和耐克(Nike)等公司承诺，将为寻求堕胎和其它在本州无法获得的医疗服务的员工支付旅费。

北部圣华金谷盛行的焰火表演在疫情爆发前曾吸引成千上万人来到加利福尼亚州的唐佩德罗湖，但也因为干旱问题而取消，包括该湖预计水位较低。

“The safety of our guests and being good stewards of the land entrusted to us are our highest priorities,” the Don Pedro Recreation Agency said in a statement.

Don Pedro 娱乐机构在一份声明中表示: “我们最优先考虑的是客人的安全和对委托给我们的土地的良好管理。”。

Lompoc on California’s central coast and Castle Rock in Colorado canceled their pyrotechnic displays over worries about wildfires. Still, an Independence Eve fireworks show with live music by the Colorado Symphony is planned July 3 at Denver’s Civic Center Park.

加利福尼亚中部海岸的隆波克和科罗拉多州的城堡岩由于担心森林大火而取消了他们的烟火表演。尽管如此，科罗拉多交响乐团还是计划于7月3日在丹佛市中心公园举行独立前夜烟火表演，并伴有现场音乐。

In New Mexico, the most destructive wildfire season in modern history won’t stop that state’s major cities, including Albuquerque and Santa Fe, from holding Fourth of July fireworks displays under fire department supervision.

在新墨西哥州，现代历史上最具破坏性的野火季节不会阻止该州的主要城市，包括阿尔伯克基和圣达菲，在消防部门的监督下举行独立日烟火表演。

The Southgate Mall in Missoula, Montana, canceled its annual Fourth of July celebration and fireworks show without giving a reason.

Southgate Mall 在米苏拉取消了一年一度的独立日庆祝活动和焰火表演，但没有给出原因。

Elsewhere in the U.S., some North Carolina towns canceled displays after a recent fireworks-related explosion killed a man on a small farm and a large cache of fireworks were destroyed in a related fire.

在美国其他地方，北卡罗来纳州的一些城镇取消了烟花表演，因为最近一起与烟花有关的爆炸导致一个小农场的一名男子死亡，大量的烟花在一场相关的火灾中被烧毁。

In Minneapolis, a fireworks display over the Mississippi won’t be held because of staff shortages and construction at a nearby park.

在明尼阿波利斯，由于工作人员短缺和附近公园的建设，密西西比河上空的烟火表演将不会举行。

Those who plan to light up consumer-grade fireworks like bottle rockets, firecrackers and ground-level fountains at home can expect to pay more for them. The American Pyrotechnic Association estimates that costs are up 35% across the industry.

那些计划在家中点燃像瓶装火箭、爆竹和地面喷泉等消费级烟花的人可以期望为此支付更多费用。美国烟火协会估计，整个行业的成本上升了35% 。

Fire officials in some cities worry that the cancelations of community displays could prompt some people to ramp up their use of consumer-grade fireworks.

一些城市的消防官员担心，取消社区烟花表演可能会促使一些人更多地使用消费级烟花爆竹。

“We are typically worried about exposure of sparks and fire to homes and dry brush,” said Phoenix Fire spokesperson Capt. Evan Gammage. “We get so many calls around this time of year.”

“我们通常担心暴露在火花和火灾的家庭和干灌木丛,”凤凰城火灾发言人埃文 Gammage 上尉说。“每年这个时候我们都会接到很多电话。”

JWST

WELL, IT WORKED. This “deep-field” picture shows that the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) is doing what it was sent up to do—taking spectacular images of the cosmos. The image shows SMACS 0723, a cluster of comparatively nearby galaxies whose gravity acts to bend and concentrate light from far, far more distant ones behind them. For the moment (for JWST has only just got going) the faintest of those “gravitationally lensed” galaxies are the most distant objects Earthlings have ever set eyes on.

起作用了。这张“深视野”照片显示，詹姆斯·韦伯空间望远镜(JWST)正在完成它被送上太空的任务ーー拍摄壮观的宇宙图像。这张图片显示的是 SMACS 0723，一个相对较近的星系团，其引力作用使来自它们身后更远更远的星系的光线发生弯曲和聚集。目前(因为 JWST 才刚刚开始) ，那些最微弱的“引力透镜”星系是地球人曾经看到过的最遥远的天体。

JWST was launched, after 11 years of delays and at a cost of $9.7bn, on Christmas Day 2021. Its ballooning budget, even when split between nasa and the space agencies of Europe and Canada, almost got it cancelled. But it was too big to be sunk. Before lift-off, Thomas Zurbuchen, nasa’s head of science, told The Economist that “the last thing we want to do is save a billion dollars and fail”.

经过11年的拖延，JWST 于2021年圣诞节当天启动，耗资97亿美元。它不断膨胀的预算，即使是由 NASA 和欧洲及加拿大的航天机构分担，也几乎让它被取消。但它太大了，不能沉没。在发射前，美国宇航局科学主管 Thomas Zurbuchen 告诉《经济学人》 ，“我们最不想做的事情就是节省10亿美元却失败了。

Seven months into the mission, though, every aspect of launch, deployment and performance seems to have gone according to plan, if not better. As a result, astronomers now have the most powerful tool yet given them to scan the cosmos in infrared frequencies of light. That will let them study many things they have struggled to examine in the past—in particular, the formation of stars and planets, from the universe’s youth, more than 13bn years ago, to the present day.

不过，在执行任务7个月后，发射、部署和性能的各个方面似乎都按计划进行，甚至更好。因此，天文学家们现在拥有了迄今为止最强大的工具，可以用红外光的频率来扫描宇宙。这将使他们能够研究过去他们一直难以探索的许多问题，尤其是恒星和行星的形成，从130多亿年前宇宙的初期到现在。

Heat and dust

热和灰尘

After its launch, the jwst manoeuvred its way to Lagrange 2 (l2), a point in space 1.5m km from Earth. At this spot the gravitational fields of Earth and sun conspire to create a gravity well. The telescope does not sit at l2. Rather, it orbits it. l2 was chosen partly because of its ability to anchor a spacecraft in this way and partly because the alignment of Earth and sun, as seen from it, means illumination from both can be blocked by a single shield. Since infrared-detecting instruments have to be kept cold, protecting them from extraneous sources of heat and light is important.

在发射后，JWST 机动地前往拉格朗日2(l2) ，一个距离地球1.5公里的太空点。在这里，地球和太阳的引力场共同创造了一个引力井。望远镜不在 l2。更确切地说，它围绕着它旋转。选择 l2的部分原因是因为它能够以这种方式固定航天器，部分原因是因为从它看来，地球和太阳的排列意味着两者的照明都可以被单一的防护罩阻挡。由于红外探测仪器必须保持低温，保护它们不受外来热源和光源的影响就显得非常重要。

On the journey to l2 the telescope’s operators unfolded its solar panels, an antenna to facilitate communication with Earth, the shield and the two mirrors that shape the images. One is a parabolic primary, 6.8 metres across, assembled out of hexagonal cells made from gold-plated beryllium. This gathers and focuses incoming electromagnetic radiation. The second is a smaller, hyperbolic secondary held in front of the primary by three struts. Using a design invented by Laurent Cassegrain, a French astronomer of the 17th century, this secondary intercepts the narrowing beam from the primary and reflects it back through a hole in the primary’s centre to four instruments.

在前往 l2的旅途中，望远镜的操作员展开了太阳能电池板，一个天线，以方便与地球的通信，护盾和形成图像的两面镜子。其中一个是抛物面质点，直径6.8米，可分为为六角形，由镀金铍制成。它收集和聚焦来自外界的电磁辐射。第二个是一个较小的，双曲线次要举行在前面的主要由三个支柱。利用17世纪法国天文学家洛朗 · 卡塞格林发明的一种设计，这种辅助装置拦截来自主光束的窄光束，并通过主光束中心的一个洞将其反射回四个仪器。

These are miri (for detecting long infrared wavelengths), nirCam and nirSpec (which take images of and analyse shortwave infrared) and fgs/niriss (which studies bright targets such as nearby stars orbited by exoplanets). The wavelengths examined by miri correspond to objects such as exoplanets that have no internal source of heat, and hotter but more distant bodies whose light has been stretched from visibility into the infrared by the expansion of the universe. Since “farther away” also means “longer ago” in cosmic terms, this will enable it to spot signs of the cosmic dawn, the moment when the universe’s first stars ignited. And, on top of these two benefits, long-wavelength infrared of the sort miri detects penetrates dust clouds more successfully than visible light can, thus tearing away the veil from intriguing pockets of the sky where dust is coalescing into stars and planets.

它们分别是 Miri (用于探测长红外波长)、 nirCam 和 nirSpec (用于拍摄和分析短波红外线)以及 fgs/niriss (用于研究系外行星轨道附近的恒星等明亮目标)。Miri 观测到的波长对应于一些物体，比如没有内部热源的系外行星，以及由于宇宙膨胀而将光从可见光拉伸到红外线的更远但更热的物体。由于“更远”在宇宙术语中也意味着“更久以前”，这将使它能够发现宇宙黎明的迹象，宇宙的第一颗恒星点燃的时刻。除了这两个好处之外，Miri 探测到的长波长红外线能够比可见光更成功地穿透尘埃云，从而揭开了天空中有趣区域的面纱，尘埃正在这些区域合并成恒星和行星。

The launch’s accuracy meant midcourse corrections needed to put the telescope in orbit used less fuel than budgeted. That leaves more for the small adjustments needed to keep the instrument on station. Since station-keeping is the main constraint on how long the mission can last, that matters. The initial goal was ten years, but nasa now reckons it can keep the telescope in place for 20. On top of this, all four instruments appear more sensitive than modelled, and thus capable of collecting 10-20% more photons than expected.

发射的准确性意味着将望远镜送入轨道所需的中途修正比预算中的燃料要少。这就留下了更多的小调整需要保持仪器在空间站。由于空间站的保持是制约任务持续时间的主要因素，这一点很重要。最初的目标是10年，但美国宇航局现在估计它可以保持望远镜在20个地方。除此之外，这四种仪器看起来比模型更加灵敏，因此能够比预期多收集10-20% 的光子。

The release of this image (rendered in full below), along with a few more that will come on Tuesday, marks the conclusion of the telescope’s commissioning, a lengthy process intended to make sure it is fit for purpose. Management will now be transferred to the Space Telescope Science Institute in Baltimore, which will have the thankless task of allocating time on it to eager astronomers. The good news is that the new estimates of its working life mean many more requests will eventually be fulfilled. The bad is that there may be a long wait. ■

1.The Commercialization of Lumber

木材的商业化

  • In nineteenth-century America, practically everything that was built involved wood.Pine was especially attractive for building purposes.It is durable and strong, yet soft enough to be easily worked with even the simplest of hand tools.It also floats nicely on water, which allowed it to be transported to distant markets across the nation.The central and northern reaches of the Great Lakes states—Michigan, Wisconsin, and Minnesota—all contained extensive pine forests as well as many large rivers for floating logs into the Great Lakes, from where they were transported nationwide.

  • 在十九世纪的美国，几乎所有建造的东西都与木材有关。松树对建筑用途特别有吸引力。它耐用且坚固，但足够柔软，即使是最简单的手动工具也可以轻松操作。它也很好地漂浮在水面上，这使得它能够被运送到全国各地的遥远市场。五大湖州的中部和北部 - 密歇根州，威斯康星州和明尼苏达州 - 都包含广泛的松树林以及许多大型河流，用于将原木漂浮到五大湖中，从那里将它们运往全国。

  • By 1860, the settlement of the American West along with timber shortages in the East converged with ever-widening impact on the pine forests of the Great Lakes states. Over the next 30 years, lumbering became a full-fledged enterprise in Michigan, Wisconsin, and Minnesota. Newly formed lumbering corporations bought up huge tracts of pineland and set about systematically cutting the trees. Both the colonists and the later industrialists saw timber as a commodity, but the latter group adopted a far more thorough and calculating approach to removing trees. In this sense, what happened between 1860 and 1890 represented a significant break with the past. No longer were farmers in search of extra income the main source for shingles, firewood, and other wood products. By the 1870s, farmers and city dwellers alike purchased forest products from large manufacturing companies located in the Great Lakes states rather than chopping wood themselves or buying it locally.

  • 到1860年，美国西部的定居以及东部的木材短缺，对五大湖区各州的松树林产生了越来越大的影响。在接下来的30年里，伐木业在密歇根州、威斯康星州和明尼苏达州成为一个全面的企业。新成立的伐木公司买下了大片的松树林，开始有计划地砍伐树木。殖民者和后来的工业家都把木材看作是一种商品，但后者在砍伐树木时采取了更彻底、更有计划的方法。在这个意义上，1860年至1890年间发生的事情代表了与过去的重大决裂。寻求额外收入的农民不再是瓦片、木柴和其他木制品的主要来源。到19世纪70年代，农民和城市居民都从位于大湖区各州的大型制造公司购买森林产品，而不是自己砍伐木材或在当地购买。

  • The commercialization of lumbering was in part the product of technological change. The early, thick saw blades tended to waste a large quantity of wood, with perhaps as much as a third of the log left behind on the floor as sawdust or scrap. In the 1870s, however, the British-invented band saw, with its thinner blade, became standard issue in the Great Lakes states’ lumber factories.Meanwhile, the rise of steam-powered mills streamlined production by allowing for the more efficient, centralized, and continuous cutting of lumber. Steam helped to automate a variety of tasks, from cutting to the carrying away of waste. Mills also employed steam to heat log ponds, preventing them from freezing and making possible year-round lumber production.

  • 伐木业的商业化在一定程度上是技术变革的产物。早期厚重的锯片往往会浪费大量的木材，可能有多达三分之一的原木作为锯末或废料留在地板上。然而，在19世纪70年代，英国发明的带锯，其较薄的锯片，成为大湖区各州木材厂的标准配置。蒸汽帮助实现了各种任务的自动化，从切割到运走废料。工厂还利用蒸汽来加热原木池，防止其结冰，使全年的木材生产成为可能。

  • For industrial lumbering to succeed, a way had to be found to neutralize the effects of the seasons on production. Traditionally, cutting took place in the winter, when snow and ice made it easier to drag logs on sleds or sleighs to the banks of streams. Once the streams and lakes thawed, workers rafted the logs to mills, where they were cut into lumber in the summer.If nature did not cooperate—if the winter proved dry and warm, if the spring thaw was delayed—production would suffer. To counter the effects of climate on lumber production, loggers experimented with a variety of techniques for transporting trees out of the woods. In the 1870s, loggers in the Great Lakes states began sprinkling water on sleigh roads, giving them an artificial ice coating to facilitate travel. The ice reduced the friction and allowed workers to move larger and heavier loads.

  • 为了使工业化伐木获得成功，必须找到一种方法来中和季节对生产的影响。传统上，伐木是在冬天进行的，那时冰雪使人们更容易用雪橇或雪橇将原木拖到溪流的岸边。一旦溪流和湖泊解冻，工人们就用木筏将原木运到工厂，在夏天将它们切割成木材。如果大自然不配合，如果冬天证明是干燥和温暖的，如果春天的解冻被推迟，生产就会受到影响。为了应对气候对木材生产的影响，伐木者尝试了各种将树木运出森林的技术。19世纪70年代，大湖区各州的伐木工开始在雪橇道路上洒水，给它们涂上一层人工冰，以方便旅行。冰层减少了摩擦力，使工人们可以搬运更大更重的货物。

  • But all the sprinkling in the world would not save a logger from the threat of a warm winter. Without snow the sleigh roads turned to mud. In the 1870s, a set of snowless winters left lumber companies to ponder ways of liberating themselves from the seasons. Railroads were one possibility.At first, the remoteness of the pine forests discouraged common carriers from laying track.But increasing lumber prices in the late 1870s combined with periodic warm, dry winters compelled loggers to turn to iron rails. By 1887, 89 logging railroads crisscrossed Michigan, transforming logging from a winter activity into a year-round one.

  • 但是，世界上所有的洒水都不能拯救伐木者免受暖冬的威胁。没有雪，雪橇路就变成了泥巴。在19世纪70年代，一系列无雪的冬天让木材公司开始思考如何将自己从季节中解放出来。铁路是一种可能性。起初，松树林的偏远使普通承运人不愿意铺设轨道，但19世纪70年代末木材价格上涨，加上周期性的温暖、干燥的冬天，迫使伐木者转向铁轨。到1887年，89条伐木铁路横贯密歇根州，将伐木从冬季活动转变为全年活动。

2.Overkill of the North American Megafauna

北美巨型动物的过度杀戮

  • Thousands of years ago, in North America’s past, all of its megafauna—large mammals such as mammoths and giant bears—disappeared. One proposed explanation for this event is that when the first Americans migrated over from Asia, they hunted the megafauna to extinction.These people, known as the Clovis society after a site where their distinctive spear points were first found, would have been able to use this food source to expand their population and fill the continent rapidly.Yet many scientists argue against this “Pleistocene overkill” hypothesis. Modern humans have certainly been capable of such drastic effects on animals, but could ancient people with little more than stone spears similarly have caused the extinction of numerous species of animals?Thirty-five genera or groups of species (and many individual species) suffered extinction in North America around 11,000 B.C., soon after the appearance and expansion of Paleo-lndians throughout the Americas (27 genera disappeared completely, and another 8 became locally extinct, surviving only outside North America).

  • 几千年前，在北美洲的过去，所有的巨型动物–大型哺乳动物，如猛犸象和巨熊–都消失了。对这一事件提出的一种解释是，当第一批美国人从亚洲迁徙过来时，他们将巨型动物猎杀到灭绝。这些人被称为克洛维斯社会，因为他们的独特矛头首次在一个地点被发现，他们本来可以利用这一食物来源来扩大他们的人口，迅速填满整个大陆。现代人当然有能力对动物产生如此剧烈的影响，但是除了石矛之外没有什么别的东西的古代人是否也能同样造成众多动物物种的灭绝呢？公元前11000年左右，在整个美洲的古印度人出现和扩张之后不久，有35个属或物种群（以及许多单个物种）在北美遭受灭绝（27个属完全消失，另外8个属局部灭绝，只在北美以外地区生存）。

  • Although the climate changed at the end of the Pleistocene, warming trends had happened before.A period of massive extinction of large mammals like that seen about 11,000 years ago had not occurred during the previous 400,000 years, despite these changes.The only apparently significant difference in the Americas 11,000 years ago was the presence of human hunters of these large mammals.Was this coincidence or cause-and-effect?

  • 尽管气候在更新世末期发生了变化，但变暖的趋势在之前就已经发生了。尽管有这些变化，像大约1.1万年前看到的大型哺乳动物大规模灭绝的时期在之前的40万年里没有发生过。1.1万年前的美洲唯一明显的区别是出现了猎杀这些大型哺乳动物的人类。这是巧合还是因果关系？

  • We do not know.Ecologist Paul S. Martin has championed the model that associates the extinction of large mammals at the end of the Pleistocene with human predation.With researcher J. E. Mosimann, he has co-authored a work in which a computer model showed that in around 300 years, given the right conditions, a small influx of hunters into eastern Beringia 12,000 years ago could have spread across North America in a wave and wiped out game animals to feed their burgeoning population.

  • 我们不知道。生态学家保罗-S-马丁（Paul S. Martin）倡导将更新世末期大型哺乳动物的灭绝与人类捕食联系起来的模式。他与研究员J-E-莫西曼（J. E. Mosimann）共同撰写了一项工作，其中一个计算机模型显示，在大约300年内，如果条件合适，12000年前涌入白令谷东部的少量猎人可能会一波流传到北美并消灭野味，以满足其膨胀的人口。

  • The researchers ran the model several ways, always beginning with a population of 100 humans in Edmonton, in Alberta, Canada, at 11,500 years ago.Assuming different initial North American big-game-animal populations (75-150 million animals) and different population growth rates for the human settlers (0.65%-3.5%), and varying kill rates, Mosimann and Martin derived figures of between 279 and 1,157 years from initial contact to big-game extinction.

  • 研究人员以多种方式运行该模型，总是从11500年前加拿大阿尔伯塔省埃德蒙顿的100个人类人口开始。假设不同的北美大型动物的初始数量（7,500-1.5亿只）和人类定居者的不同人口增长率（0.65%-3.5%），以及不同的杀戮率，莫西曼和马丁得出从最初接触到大型动物灭绝的数字为279到1157年。

  • Many scholars continue to support this scenario.For example, geologist Larry Agenbroad has mapped the locations of dated Clovis sites alongside the distribution of dated sites where the remains of wooly mammoths have been found in both archaeological and purely paleontological contexts.These distributions show remarkable synchronicity (occurrence at the same time).

  • 许多学者继续支持这一设想。例如，地质学家拉里-阿根布罗德（Larry Agenbroad）将有日期的克洛维斯遗址的位置与在考古学和纯古生物学背景下发现长毛象遗骸的有日期的遗址的分布绘制在一起。这些分布显示出显著的同步性（同时发生）。

  • There are, however, many problems with this model.Significantly, though a few sites are quite impressive, there really is very little archaeological evidence to support it.Writing in 1982, Martin himself admitted the paucity of evidence;for example, at that point, the remains of only 38 individual mammoths had been found at Clovis sites. In the years since, few additional mammoths have been added to the list;there are still fewer than 20 Clovis sites where the remains of one or more mammoths have been recovered, a minuscule proportion of the millions that necessarily would have had to have been slaughtered within the overkill scenario.

  • 然而，这种模式有很多问题。重要的是，虽然有几个遗址令人印象深刻，但确实没有什么考古学证据来支持它。1982年，马丁自己也承认了证据的匮乏；例如，当时在克洛维斯遗址只发现了38头单个猛犸象的遗骸。在此后的几年里，很少有新的猛犸象被添加到名单中；目前仍有不到20个克洛维斯遗址发现了一只或多只猛犸象的遗骸，这与在过度杀戮的情况下必然要被屠杀的数百万猛犸象相比，只是很小的一部分。

  • Though Martin claims the lack of evidence actually supports his model—the evidence is sparse because the spread of humans and the extinction of animals occurred so quickly—this argument seems weak. And how could we ever disprove it?As archaeologist Donald Grayson points out, in other cases where extinction resulted from the quick spread of human hunters—for example, the extinction of the moa, the large flightless bird of New Zealand—archaeological evidence in the form of remains is abundant. Grayson has also shown that the evidence is not so clear that all or even most of the large herbivores in late Pleistocene America became extinct after the appearance of Clovis. Of the 35 extinct genera, only 8 can be confidently assigned an extinction date of between 12,000 and 10,000 years ago.Many of the older genera, Grayson argues, may have succumbed before 12,000 B.C., at least half a century before the Clovis showed up in the American West.

  • 尽管马丁声称，证据的缺乏实际上支持了他的模型–证据稀少是因为人类的传播和动物的灭绝发生得太快了–这一论点似乎很弱。正如考古学家Donald Grayson所指出的，在其他因人类猎人的快速传播而导致灭绝的案例中–例如，新西兰的大型不飞鸟–Moa的灭绝–以遗骸形式存在的考古学证据非常丰富。格雷森还表明，证据并不十分清楚，晚更新世美洲的所有或甚至大多数大型食草动物在克洛维斯出现后就灭绝了。在35个灭绝的属中，只有8个可以有把握地将灭绝日期定在12000年至10000年前。格雷森认为，许多较早的属可能在公元前12000年之前就已经屈服了，至少在克洛维斯出现在美国西部的半个世纪之前。

3.Elements of Life

生命的元素

  • The creation of life requires a set of chemical elements for making the components of cells. Life on Earth uses about 25 of the 92 naturally occurring chemical elements, although just 4 of these elements—oxygen, carbon, hydrogen, and nitrogen—make up about 96 percent of the mass of living organisms. Thus, a first requirement for life might be the presence of most or all of the elements used by life.

  • 生命的创造需要一套化学元素来制造细胞的组成部分。地球上的生命使用92种自然发生的化学元素中的大约25种，尽管这些元素中只有4种–氧、碳、氢和氮–占了生物体质量的大约96%。因此，生命的第一个要求可能是存在生命所使用的大部分或全部元素。

  • Interestingly, this requirement can probably be met by almost any world. Scientists have determined that all chemical elements in the universe besides hydrogen and helium (and a trace amount of lithium) were produced by stars. These are known as heavy elements because they are heavier than hydrogen and helium. Although all of these heavy elements are quite rare compared to hydrogen and helium, they are found just about everywhere.

  • 有趣的是，这个要求可能几乎可以由任何世界来满足。科学家已经确定，宇宙中除了氢和氦（以及微量的锂）之外的所有化学元素都是由恒星产生的。这些被称为重元素，因为它们比氢和氦更重。尽管与氢和氦相比，所有这些重元素都相当罕见，但它们几乎到处都能找到。

  • Heavy elements are continually being manufactured by stars and released into space by stellar deaths, so their amount compared to hydrogen and helium gradually rises with time. Heavy elements make up about 2 percent of the chemical content (by mass) of our solar system, the other 98 percent is hydrogen and helium. In some very old star systems, which formed before many heavy elements were produced, the heavy-element share may be less than 0.1 percent. Nevertheless, every star system studied has at least some amount of all the elements used by life.Moreover, when planetesimals—small, solid objects formed in the early solar system that may accumulate to become planets—condense within a forming star system, they are inevitably made from heavy elements because the more common hydrogen and helium remain gaseous.Thus, planetesimals everywhere should contain the elements needed for life, which means that objects built from planetesimals—planets, moons, asteroids, and comets-also contain these elements. The nature of solar-system formation explains why Earth contains all the elements needed for life, and it is why we expect these elements to be present on other worlds throughout our solar system, galaxy, and universe.

  • 重元素不断地被恒星制造出来，并通过恒星的死亡释放到太空中，因此它们的数量与氢和氦相比，随着时间的推移逐渐上升。重元素在我们太阳系的化学成分（按质量）中约占2%，其他98%是氢和氦。在一些非常古老的恒星系统中，它们在许多重元素产生之前就已经形成了，重元素的份额可能低于0.1%。此外，当小行星–在早期太阳系中形成的、可能积累成行星的小型固体物体–在形成中的恒星系统中凝结时，它们不可避免地由重元素构成，因为更常见的氢和氦仍然是气态的。因此，各地的小行星应该含有生命所需的元素，这意味着由小行星建造的物体–行星、卫星、小行星和彗星–也含有这些元素。太阳系形成的性质解释了为什么地球包含生命所需的所有元素，这也是为什么我们预计这些元素会存在于整个太阳系、银河系和宇宙的其他世界。

  • Note that this argument does not change, even if we allow for life very different from life on Earth. Life on Earth is carbon based, and most biologists believe that life elsewhere is likely to be carbon based as well. However, we cannot absolutely rule out the possibility of life with another chemical basis, such as silicon or nitrogen. The set of elements (or their relative proportions) used by life based on some other element might be somewhat different from that used by carbon-based life on Earth. But the elements are still products of stars and would still be present in planetesimals everywhere. No matter what kinds of life we are looking for, we are likely to find the necessary elements on almost every planet, moon, asteroid, and comet in the universe.

  • 请注意，即使我们允许有与地球上的生命非常不同的生命，这个论点也不会改变。地球上的生命是以碳为基础的，而且大多数生物学家认为，其他地方的生命也可能是以碳为基础的。然而，我们不能绝对排除有另一种化学基础的生命的可能性，如硅或氮。基于其他一些元素的生命所使用的元素集（或其相对比例）可能与地球上的碳基生命所使用的元素集有些不同。但是这些元素仍然是恒星的产物，仍然会存在于各地的行星上。无论我们在寻找什么样的生命，我们都有可能在宇宙中几乎每一颗行星、月球、小行星和彗星上找到必要的元素。

  • A somewhat stricter requirement is the presence of these elements in molecules that can be used as ready-made building blocks for life, just as early Earth probably had an organic soup of amino acids and other complex molecules. Earth’s organic molecules likely came from some combination of three sources: chemical reactions in the atmosphere, chemical reactions near deep-sea vents in the oceans, and molecules carried to Earth by asteroids and comets. The first two sources can occur only on worlds with atmospheres or oceans, respectively. But the third source should have brought similar molecules to nearly all worlds in our solar system.

  • 一个更严格的要求是，这些元素存在于可以作为生命的现成构件的分子中，就像早期地球可能有氨基酸和其他复杂分子的有机汤。地球上的有机分子可能来自三个来源的某种组合：大气中的化学反应，海洋中深海喷口附近的化学反应，以及由小行星和彗星携带到地球的分子。前两个来源只可能分别发生在有大气层或海洋的世界上。但是第三个来源应该把类似的分子带到我们太阳系的几乎所有世界。

  • Studies of meteorites and comets suggest that organic molecules are widespread among both asteroids and comets. Because each body in the solar system was repeatedly struck by asteroids and comets during the period known as the heavy bombardment (about 4 billion years ago), each body should have received at least some organic molecules. However, these molecules tend to be destroyed by solar radiation on surfaces unprotected by atmospheres. Moreover, while these molecules might stay intact beneath the surface (as they evidently do on asteroids and comets), they probably cannot react with each other unless some kind of liquid or gas is available to move them about. Thus, if we limit our search to worlds on which organic molecules are likely to be involved in chemical reactions, we can probably rule out any world that lacks both an atmosphere and a surface or subsurface liquid medium, such as water.

  • 对陨石和彗星的研究表明，有机分子在小行星和彗星中都很普遍。由于太阳系中的每个天体在被称为重度轰击的时期（大约40亿年前）被小行星和彗星反复撞击，每个天体至少应该得到一些有机分子。然而，这些分子在没有大气层保护的表面上往往会被太阳辐射破坏。此外，虽然这些分子可能在表面下保持完整（正如它们在小行星和彗星上显然所做的那样），但除非有某种液体或气体可以移动它们，否则它们可能无法相互反应。因此，如果我们把搜索范围限制在有机分子有可能参与化学反应的世界上，我们大概可以排除任何既没有大气层又没有表面或地下液体介质的世界，比如水。

4.Evidence of the Earliest Writing

最早书写的证据

  • Although literacy appeared independently in several parts of the prehistoric world, the earliest evidence of writing is the cuneiform Sumerian script on the clay tablets of ancient Mesopotamia, which, archaeological detective work has revealed, had its origins in the accounting practices of commercial activity. Researchers demonstrated that preliterate people, to keep track of the goods they produced and exchanged, created a system of accounting using clay tokens as symbolic representations of their products. Over many thousands of years, the symbols evolved through several stages of abstraction until they became wedge- shaped (cuneiform) signs on clay tablets, recognizable as writing.

  • 尽管识字在史前世界的几个地方独立出现，但最早的文字证据是古代美索不达米亚的泥板上的楔形苏美尔文字，考古探测工作显示，这种文字起源于商业活动的会计做法。研究人员证明，识字前的人们为了记录他们生产和交换的货物，创造了一个使用粘土令牌作为其产品的象征性代表的会计系统。几千年来，这些符号经历了几个抽象阶段，直到它们成为泥板上的楔形符号（楔形文字），可被识别为文字。

  • The original tokens (circa 8500 B.C.E.) were three-dimensional solid shapes—tiny spheres, cones, disks, and cylinders. A debt of six units of grain and eight head of livestock, for example might have been represented by six conical and eight cylindrical tokens. To keep batches of tokens together, an innovation was introduced (circa 3250 B. C. E.) whereby they were sealed inside clay envelopes that could be broken open and counted when it came time for a debt to be repaid. But because the contents of the envelopes could easily be forgotten, two-dimensional representations of the three-dimensional tokens were impressed into the surface of the envelopes before they were sealed. Eventually, having two sets of equivalent symbols—the internal tokens and external markings—came to seem redundant, so the tokens were eliminated (circa 3250-3100 B.C.E.), and only solid clay tablets with two-dimensional symbols were retained. Over time, the symbols became more numerous, varied, and abstract and came to represent more than trade commodities, evolving eventually into cuneiform writing.

  • 最初的代币（约公元前8500年）是三维的固体形状–小球体、圆锥体、圆盘和圆柱体。例如，6个单位的谷物和8头牲畜的债务可能由6个圆锥体和8个圆柱体代币来表示。为了将成批的代币保存在一起，人们引入了一项创新（大约在公元前3250年），将它们密封在泥土信封里，当需要偿还债务时，可以打开信封进行清点。但由于信封里的东西很容易被遗忘，所以在密封之前，三维代币的二维表现被印在信封的表面。最终，拥有两套等效的符号–内部代币和外部标记–似乎是多余的，所以代币被取消了（大约在公元前3250-3100年），只保留了带有二维符号的固体泥板。随着时间的推移，这些符号变得越来越多，越来越多，越来越抽象，而且不仅仅代表贸易商品，最终演变为楔形文字。

  • The evolution of the symbolism is reflected in the archaeological record first of all by the increasing complexity of the tokens themselves. The earliest tokens, dating from about 10,000 to 6,000 years ago, were of only the simplest geometric shapes. But about 3500 B.C.E., more complex tokens came into common usage, including many naturalistic forms shaped like miniature tools, furniture, fruit, and humans. The earlier, plain tokens were counters for agricultural products, whereas the complex ones stood for finished products, such as bread, oil, perfume, wool, and rope, and for items produced in workshops, such as metal, bracelets, types of cloth, garments, mats, pieces of furniture, tools, and a variety of stone and pottery vessels. The signs marked on clay tablets likewise evolved from simple wedges, circles, ovals, and triangles based on the plain tokens to pictographs derived from the complex tokens.

  • 象征主义的演变首先反映在考古记录中，就是令牌本身的复杂性不断增加。最早的代币，可以追溯到大约1万到6千年前，只有最简单的几何形状。但大约在公元前3500年，更复杂的代币开始被普遍使用，包括许多自然形态的微型工具、家具、水果和人类的形状。早期的普通符号是农产品的计数器，而复杂的符号则代表成品，如面包、油、香水、羊毛和绳子，以及工场生产的物品，如金属、手镯、各类布、服装、垫子、家具、工具和各种石器和陶器。泥板上标记的符号也同样从简单的楔形、圆形、椭圆形和三角形发展到从复杂符号中衍生出的象形文字。

  • Before this evidence came to light, the inventors of writing were assumed by researchers to have been an intellectual elite. Some, for example, hypothesized that writing emerged when members of the priestly caste agreed among themselves on written signs. But the association of the plain tokens with the first farmers and of the complex tokens with the first artisans—and the fact that the token-and-envelope accounting system invariably represented only small-scale transactions—testifies to the relatively modest social status of the creators of writing.

  • 在这些证据出现之前，研究人员认为文字的发明者是一个知识精英。例如，有些人假设，文字是在祭司阶层成员之间就书面符号达成一致时出现的。但是，将普通代币与第一批农民联系起来，将复杂的代币与第一批工匠联系起来，以及代币和信封会计系统总是只代表小规模的交易这一事实，证明了文字创造者的社会地位相对较低。

  • And not only of literacy, but numeracy (the representation of quantitative concepts) as well. The evidence of the tokens provides further confirmation that mathematics originated in people’s desire to keep records of flocks and other goods. Another immensely significant step occurred around 3100 B.C.E., when Sumerian accountants extended the token-based signs to include the first real numerals. Previously, units of grain had been represented by direct one-to-one correspondence―by repeating the token or symbol for a unit of grain the required number of times. The accountants, however, devised numeral signs distinct from commodity signs, so that eighteen units of grain could be indicated by preceding a single grain symbol with a symbol denoting “18.” Their invention of abstract numerals and abstract counting was one of the most revolutionary advances in the history of mathematics.

  • 而且不仅是识字，还有算术（数量概念的表述）。代币的证据进一步证实，数学起源于人们对记录羊群和其他物品的渴望。另一个极其重要的步骤发生在公元前3100年左右，当时苏美尔的会计人员将基于令牌的符号扩展到包括第一个真正的数字。在此之前，谷物单位是通过直接的一对一的对应关系来表示的–即重复一个谷物单位的标记或符号的必要次数。然而，会计人员设计了与商品符号不同的数字符号，因此18个单位的谷物可以通过在一个谷物符号前加上一个表示 “18 “的符号来表示。他们发明的抽象数字和抽象计数是数学史上最具革命性的进步之一。

  • What was the social status of the anonymous accountants who produced this breakthrough? The immense volume of clay tablets unearthed in the ruins of the Sumerian temples where the accounts were kept suggests a social differentiation within the scribal class, with a virtual army of lower-ranking tabulators performing the monotonous job of tallying commodities. We can only speculate as to how high or low the inventors of true numerals were in the scribal hierarchy, but it stands to reason that this laborsaving innovation would have been the brainchild of the lower-ranking types whose drudgery is eased.

  • 产生这一突破的匿名会计人员的社会地位如何？在保存账目的苏美尔神庙废墟中出土的大量泥板表明，文士阶层内部存在着社会分化，实际上有一支由低级制表人组成的军队在从事统计商品的单调工作。我们只能猜测真数字的发明者在文士阶层中的地位有多高或多低，但有理由相信，这种节省劳动力的创新应该是低级别的人的创意，他们的苦差事被减轻了。

功能描述：通过deepl的api实现英译中，将英文与中文拼接，重新写入新的txt文件
具体效果可见：

#01.实现deepl翻译，封装功能函数
#deepl_txt.py

import requests
from fastlid import fastlid


def Translator_deepl(text):

    lang = fastlid(text)
    if lang == "zh":
        from_lang, to_lang = "zh", "en"
    elif lang == "en":
        from_lang, to_lang = "en", "zh"
    else:
        from_lang = lang[0]
        to_lang = "zh"

    data = [text, from_lang, to_lang]
    json_data = {"data": data}

    url = "https://hf.space/embed/mikeee/gradio-deepl/+/api/predict"
    resp = requests.post(url, json=json_data)
    trans = resp.json()


    return (f"译:{trans['data'][0]}")

Because of industrialization, but also because of a vast increase in agricultural output without which industrialization would have been impossible, Western Europeans by the latter half of the nineteenth century enjoyed higher standards of living and longer, healthier lives than most of the world’s peoples
由于工业化，同时也由于农业产出的大量增加，没有农业产出就不可能实现工业化，到19世纪后半期，西欧人比世界上大多数人享有更高的生活水平和更长、更健康的生命。
In Europe as a whole, the population rose from 188 million in 1800 to 400 million in 1900
在整个欧洲，人口从1800年的1.88亿上升到1900年的4亿。
By 1900, virtually every area of Europe had contributed to the tremendous surge of population, but each major region was at a different stage of demographic change
到1900年，欧洲几乎每个地区都为人口的巨大激增做出了贡献，但每个主要地区都处于人口变化的不同阶段。

Improvements in the food supply continued trends that had started in the late seventeenth century
食品供应的改善延续了17世纪末开始的趋势
New lands were put under cultivation, while the use of crops of American origin, particularly the potato, continued to expand
新的土地被投入耕种，而源自美国的作物，特别是马铃薯的使用继续扩大。
Setbacks did occur
确实发生了挫折 确实出现了挫折 挫折确实发生了 确实有挫折发生
Regional agricultural failures were the most common cause of economic recessions until 1850, and they could lead to localized famine as well
在1850年之前，地区性的农业失败是经济衰退最常见的原因，它们也可能导致局部的饥荒。
A major potato blight (disease) in 1846-1847 led to the deaths of at least one million persons in Ireland and the emigration of another million, and Ireland never recovered the population levels the potato had sustained to that point
1846-1847年的一次重大的马铃薯枯萎病（疾病）导致爱尔兰至少有100万人死亡，另有100万人移居国外，爱尔兰再也没有恢复到马铃薯到那时所维持的人口水平
Bad grain harvests at the same time led to increased hardship throughout much of Europe
同时，粮食歉收导致整个欧洲大部分地区的困苦加剧

After 1850, however, the expansion of foods more regularly kept pace with population growth, though the poorer classes remained malnourished
然而，在1850年之后，食品的扩张更经常地与人口增长保持同步，尽管较贫穷的阶层仍然营养不良。
Two developments were crucial
有两个发展是至关重要的 有两项发展是至关重要的 有两个发展是关键的 有两个事态发展是至关重要的
First, the application of science and new technology to agriculture increased
首先，科学和新技术在农业中的应用增加了
Led by German universities, increasing research was devoted to improving seeds, developing chemical fertilizers, and advancing livestock
在德国大学的带领下，越来越多的研究致力于改进种子、开发化肥和推进畜牧业的发展。
After 1861, with the development of land-grant universities in the United States that had huge agricultural programs, American crop-production research added to this mix
1861年后，随着美国拥有庞大农业项目的赠地大学的发展，美国的作物生产研究也加入了这一组合。
Mechanization included the use of horse-drawn harvesters and seed drills, many developed initially in the United States
机械化包括使用马拉的收割机和播种机，其中许多最初是在美国开发的。
It also included mechanical cream separators and other food-processing devices that improved supply
它还包括机械奶油分离器和其他改善供应的食品加工装置。

The second development involved industrially based transportation
第二个发展涉及基于工业的运输
With trains and steam shipping, it became possible to move foods to needy regions within Western Europe quickly
有了火车和蒸汽船，就有可能将食物迅速运送到西欧的贫困地区。
Famine (as opposed to malnutrition) became a thing of the past
饥荒（相对于营养不良）成为过去的事情
Many Western European countries, headed by Britain, began also to import increasing amounts of food, not only from Eastern Europe, a traditional source, but also from the Americas, Australia, and New Zealand
以英国为首的许多西欧国家也开始进口越来越多的食品，不仅从东欧这个传统来源，而且还从美洲、澳大利亚和新西兰进口。
Steam shipping, which improved speed and capacity, as well as new procedures for canning and refrigerating foods (particularly after 1870), was fundamental to these developments
蒸汽运输提高了速度和容量，以及罐装和冷藏食品的新程序（特别是在1870年之后），是这些发展的基础。

Europe’s population growth included one additional innovation by the nineteenth century: it combined with rapid urbanization
到19世纪，欧洲的人口增长还包括一项创新：它与快速的城市化相结合
More and more Western Europeans moved from countryside to city, and big cities grew most rapidly of all
越来越多的西欧人从乡村迁往城市，而大城市的发展最为迅速
By 1850, over half of all the people in England lived in cities, a first in human history
到1850年，英国一半以上的人生活在城市，这是人类历史上的第一次。
In one sense, this pattern seems inevitable growing numbers of people pressed available resources on the land, even when farmwork was combined with a bit of manufacturing, so people crowded into cities seeking work or other resources
从某种意义上说，这种模式似乎是不可避免的，越来越多的人压榨着土地上的可用资源，即使是在农业劳动与一些制造业相结合的情况下，人们也会挤进城市寻找工作或其他资源。
Traditionally, however, death rates in cities surpassed those in the countryside by a large margin; cities had maintained population only through steady in-migration
然而，传统上，城市的死亡率远远超过了农村的死亡率；城市只能通过稳定的移民来维持人口。
Thus rapid urbanization should have reduced overall population growth, but by the middle of the nineteenth century this was no longer the case
因此，快速的城市化应该减少总的人口增长，但到了19世纪中期，情况就不再是这样了
Urban death rates remained high, particularly in the lower-class slums, but they began to decline rapidly
城市的死亡率仍然很高，特别是在下层贫民窟，但它们开始迅速下降

The greater reliability of food supplies was a factor in the decline of urban death rates
食物供应更加可靠是城市死亡率下降的一个因素
Even more important were the gains in urban sanitation, as well as measures such as inspection of housing
更重要的是在城市卫生方面取得的成果，以及对住房进行检查等措施。
Reformers, including enlightened doctors, began to study the causes of high death rates and to urge remediation
改革者，包括开明的医生，开始研究高死亡率的原因，并敦促进行补救。
Even before the discovery of germs, beliefs that disease spread by “miasmas” (noxious forms of bad air) prompted attention to sewers and open garbage; Edwin Chadwick led an exemplary urban crusade for underground sewers in England in the 1830s
甚至在发现病菌之前，认为疾病通过 “瘴气”（有毒的不良空气形式）传播的信念就促使人们关注下水道和露天垃圾；埃德温-查德威克在19世纪30年代领导了一场关于英国地下下水道的模范城市运动。
Gradually, public health provisions began to cut into customary urban mortality rates
渐渐地，公共卫生规定开始削减城市的习惯死亡率
By 1900, in some parts of Western Europe life expectancy in the cities began to surpass that of the rural areas
到1900年，在西欧的一些地区，城市的预期寿命开始超过农村地区的预期寿命。
Industrial societies had figured out ways to combine large and growing cities with population growth, a development that would soon spread to other parts of the world
工业社会已经想出了将大型和不断增长的城市与人口增长相结合的方法，这一发展很快就会蔓延到世界其他地区。
